Destinations

Personalized destinations can be stored as variables. Here, you can enter a word or phrase to be used as a variable and an address to that location.
For example, you may want to add a variable "work" and the address of your workplace. This way, when you are logged in, you may say "Traffic to work", and the mirror will display personalized traffic data for your work address.
In the event of a overlap between default settings in config.js and with the current logged in user, the personalized login info for that user will take priority.
